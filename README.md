# Hypercube
A 4D hypercube projected on the screen with freecam movement

## Controls
### Movement
W, A, S, D, LShift, Space for classic movement in 3D : forwards, left, backwards, right, down, up

Q and E to move "in" and "out" (in 4D)

### Rotations
Mouse and mouse wheel to look around in 3D

Control + Mouse and mouse wheel to look around in 4D
